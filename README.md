Welcome to Dr. Abey Gordon Dental Clinic Management app, developed by University of Makati students, modernizes operations at the Pioneer Street clinic in Mandaluyong. It streamlines appointment scheduling, reduces wait times, and provides a map for easy clinic location. The app enhances efficiency with digital records, improves patient-clinic communication, and increases visibility through social media integration and feedback features. By using reusable software components, it ensures flexibility and adaptability, ultimately enhancing service quality, operational efficiency, and patient satisfaction.

Application Overview

The app allows patients to conveniently book appointments from their smartphones, reducing wait times and enhancing productivity. Key features include precise appointment details, a clinic locator map, reusable software components for easy updates, and improved UI design for better user experience. Additionally, social media integration and a feedback page enhance communication, service quality, and customer satisfaction.

Installation Instructions

Recommended System Requirements:

Platform: Android

Operating system: Android 10

RAM : 3 GB

Device Storage: 32 GB

Installation

Open the Google play Store on your device.
Search for “Abey dental clinic app”
Find the game in the search results, It will be displayed with its official icon and name.
Tap "Install" or "Get" to download and install the app.


Getting Started

For Patient

Sign up

Open the app and select "Sign Up"
Enter your personal information: Name, Address, Mobile number, Birthdate, Gender, Email, and Password.
Review the terms and conditions of using the platform. This may include policies on privacy, data usage, and user conduct.
If you agree to the terms, check the box or click the "I have read the policy" button.
Click the “I Agree” button to proceed.
Log in using your email and password.
Login

Open the app and Input your Registered Email and Password
Click the “Login” button to proceed.
Viewing and Editing Profile

Tap on the profile icon at the tool bar
Click the edit icon on the upper right next to the profile picture
Take a look at your personal details and Edit to update information.
Click the “Save changes” button before exiting.
Change Password

Go to Profile
At the bottom of the page. Click the “Change password” button.
Enter your new password
Enter your current password, then your new password twice to confirm.
Save the new password.
Forgot Password

Go to the login page and find the "Forgot Password" option.
You can select which contact details to use to reset your password either via SMS or via Email.
Await a confirmation code.
Input the Confirmation code then click the “confirm” button.
Input and confirm your new password. Click “update” to save new password

Appointment Management

Booking an Appointment

Tap on “Services” from the toolbar
Select which service you need. Then click the “Book Now” button
Choose the preferred Date and session. Then Click “Next” to confirm the appointment.
Wait for your appointment to be approved
Viewing Notifications

Click the bell button in the upper right corner.
Take a look at a list of the approved appointments.
Feedback

Click the Feedback icon from the toolbar located at the bottom.
Choose the preferred emoji to the questions
Type your ratings to the dentist and Suggestions.

Admin User Profile

Viewing and Editing Profile

Tap on the profile icon at the tool bar
Click the icon on the upper right next to the profile picture
View your personal details and Edit to update information.
Click the “Save changes” button before exiting.
Viewing Appointments

Click the “Schedule” icon from the toolbar below
View all the appointment for the day.
If you click “Arrived”, Patient appointment will go to the Queue page.
If you click “Cancel”, Patient appointment will be cancelled.
Approving appointments

Click the “Approval” icon from the toolbar below.
View all the appointments that need to be approved or rescheduled
If you click “Approve”, There will be a pop message confirming the approval of the patient appointment.
Walk-ins

Click the “Schedule” icon below.
Click the “ Walk in “ button.
Input the Walk-in patient information, including Name, Address, Mobile number, Birthdate, Gender, Date, and Service.
Select session then “Confirm appointment’ to save Appointment. Otherwise “Back”
Queue

Click the “Queue” icon from the toolbar below.
View all the appointment queue.
Click “Completed” when the appointment is done.
Prescription

Click the “Queue” icon from the toolbar below
Click the prescription icon below the “Completed button”
Input the prescription needed for the patient.
Click done to save.
View History

Click the menu bar icon located at the upper right corner.
Select the History button.
View the History of patients.
View patient profile

Click the menu bar icon located at the upper right corner.
Select the History button.
Click the “View profile” button.
View Feedback

Click the “Feedback” icon from the toolbar below.
View the list of patients' feedback.
Select which user to see their feedback.
